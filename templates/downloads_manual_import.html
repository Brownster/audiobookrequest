How it works

  • With Metadata Search: We search Audible to find your book, fetch complete metadata (authors, narrators, series), apply it to tags, download cover art, and process just like a normal download.
  • Legacy Import: We use path-based parsing for title/author only (no narrators or series). Covers are not fetched.
  • Files are copied/merged into your configured library paths (audiobooks: DOWNLOAD_DIR, ebooks: BOOK_DIR).
  • Imported items appear in Downloads history as provider manual.
